Erlang's questline requires the completion of several chapters. Also by completing his questline and then defeating The Great Sage's Broken Shell, players will obtain Free Mind relic in New Game+ and also unlock the true ending of the game.

First you will need to defeat Elder Jinchi in the secret area Ancient Guanyin Temple in Black Wind Mountain.

Second you will need to defeat Fuban in the secret area Kingdom of Sahali in Yellow Wind Ridge.

Third you will need to do the Treasure Hunter's questline in The New West and defeat Green-Capped Martialist.

Fourth you will need to defeat The Duskveil in the secret area Purple Cloud Mountain in The Webbed Hollow.

And finally you will need to defeat Bishui Golden-Eyed Beast in the secret area Bishui Cave in Flaming Mountains.

After that, from The New West's Pagoda Realm's Snow-Veiled Trail Shrine, take the right and cross the bridge. Continue following the path and at the end there will be a big area with a closed large gate. Interact with it and you will be teleported to the Great Pagoda. Now head up the stairs and at the top you will find Maitreya. A cutscene will then start and you will be teleported to Mount Mei.

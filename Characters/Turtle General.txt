Once the Destined One reaches Bitter Lake, he will find himself on the back of Turtle General. With Zhu Bajie, they will land on the North Shore of Bitter Lake. Then following the beach line to the far right, they will discover a giant snake skeleton. After interacting with it, Zhu Bajie will inform the Turtle General that Snake General had passed away. Turtle General will then cry and drop a green tear. Zhu Bajie then tells the Destined One collect it as its precious. Upon doing so he wil obtain Turtle Tear.

From the Black Wind Mountain, Bamboo Grove, "Marsh of White Mist" shrine, just head ahead, you will come to a wide open water area. Whiteclad Noble is located there.

Upon its defeat he will drop Jade Fang.

On his left, within the valley, lies the easiest to forget third bell. Ringing it unlocks the hidden level Ancient Guanyin Temple, where after battling Elder Jinchi, you can obtain the Fireproof Mantle.

From Furnace Valley's The Emerald Hall Shrine, just proceed forward and a cutscene will start that will lead to a fight with Keeper of the Flaming Mountains, and he relies on summoning guais in between phases. After the body of Ma Tianba is defeated, he drops the Yin-Yang Fish to have it fight against the Destined One instead. Following his defeat, he disappears from the story, but is mentioned to have given Red Boy a false death talisman.

Upon his defeat, the Keeper of the Flaming Mountains will drop Celestial Ribbon, Fine Gold Thread x2, and Yin-Yang Daoist Robe.
